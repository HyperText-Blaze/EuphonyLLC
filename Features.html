FEATURES OF EUPHONY.

Euphony has many different commands and features, they are listed below.

Features of Euphony:

- With the use of Lavaplayer, Euphony provides you with stability, this means you'll never experience a lag in music!

- Euphony even has Basic Moderator commands to really punish the rule-breakers.

- You can also get the lyrics of the current playing songs easily with the help of Euphony!

- Euphony is a good and easy to use music bot with the best commands and better presentation

- You can repeatedly play songs with the help of Loop command.

- With search command you can search any song available on YouTube and add it to your queue.

- Use AFK command to pause music automatically for you when you are not near your mobile/PC.

And many more features!

Commands of Euphony:

MUSIC commands:

-play: Plays the requested song!

-search: Searches for a song.

-playlist: Plays a playlist from YouTube.

-pause: Pauses all the current playing songs.

-resume: Resumes the paused songs.

-queue: Shows the songs in the queue.

-lyrics: Shows the lyrics of the played song.

-loop: Loops the entire queue.

-np: Shows the current playing song.

-volume: Shows the server volume.

-skip: Skips the current song.

-stop: Stops the playback and ends the queue.

-shuffle: Shuffles the entire queue.

-skipto: Skips to a specific song.

-remove: Removes a song from the queue.

-afk: Keeps the bot in VC 24/7.

MODERATION commands:

-ban: Bans a user from the server or outside the server.

-kick: Kicks a user from the server.

-warn: Warns a user in the server.

INFO commands:

-user-info: Shows advanced statistics about a user.

-stats: Shows the bots statistics.

-ping: Shows the bots latency.

-support: Shows the developer contact details.

-invite: Shows the bots Invite link.

-help: Shows the help message of the bot.

-vote: Shows the bot voting message.
